Site Plan

1.Site Name

Wayra Adventures • This name represents a travel platform that encourages exploration, where Wayra (the wind) is the guide, leading you to breathtaking landscapes and hidden gems. It symbolizes freedom, movement, and the thrill of discovery, ideal for adventurers who seek the journey as much as the destination.
Domain: wayra-adventures.com

2.Site Purpose

• The site provides a hub for discovering the best tourist destinations and the optimal times to visit them. It offers recommendations for scenic spots, hidden gems, and provides guidance on when to experience each location at its best, allowing travelers to plan their journeys with the freedom of the wind as their guide.

3.Scenarios

What are the best destinations to visit in Bolivia during the dry season?
Answer: The site will provide recommendations for top tourist spots to visit during Bolivia’s dry season (May to October), including festivals, hiking trails, and places with the best weather conditions.

When is the best time to visit the Salar de Uyuni to see it in its full glory?
Answer: The site will suggest the best times to visit the Salar de Uyuni, focusing on the ideal months for seeing the salt flats in their mirror-like beauty after a rain or during the dry season for vast, endless landscapes.

What are the best places to visit in Cochabamba during the summer months?
Answer: The site will highlight tourist spots in Cochabamba that are ideal for summer, such as parks, cultural events, and outdoor activities that take advantage of the sunny weather.

4.Color Schema

The color #05668D will be used as the background color.
The text color #DBF227, paired with the background #05668D, ensures clear readability and a visually appealing contrast.
Additional colors will be strategically applied throughout the website as needed to enhance the overall design and user experience.

5.Typography

Cantora
This will be the primary font used for titles, ensuring a strong and distinctive visual presence.
Nunito
This will serve as the secondary font, optimized for readability in paragraphs and body text.
